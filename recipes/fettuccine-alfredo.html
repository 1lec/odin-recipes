Fettuccine Alfredo

Description

A Italian fettuccine pasta dish topped with a creamy, cheesy alfredo sauce.

Ingredients

  • Fettuccine pasta
  • Butter
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Heavy cream
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Boil water in a large pot with a touch of salt
  2. Add pasta to the boiling water and cook it
  3. Saute garlic and butter for up to 2 minutes
  4. Add heavy cream allow to simmer for 5 minutes on medium heat
  5. Add half the parmesan and stir until smooth
  6. Top the pasta with the alredo sauce.
  7. Add half the parmesan cheese and stir until smooth
