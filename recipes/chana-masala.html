Chana Masala

Description

A delicious Indian curry made with chana, or chickpea.

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Cumin seeds
  • Bay leaf
  • Turmeric powder
  • Red chili powder
  • Coriander Powder
  • Chana masala spice powder
  • Coriander leaves
  • Kasoori methi

Steps

  1. Pressure cook chickpeas with 3 cups of water a little salt in and instant pot on high pressure for 30 minutes
  2. Add your ingredients into the pot
  3. Cook your ingredients
  4. Enjoy!
